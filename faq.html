Frequently asked questions about thermomix

Question 1: What is a Thermomix?

A Thermomix is a multifunctional kitchen appliance that can perform multiple cooking tasks, including chopping, beating, mixing, emulsifying, milling, kneading, cooking, steaming, and weighing food.

Question 2: Is the Thermomix easy to use?

Yes, the Thermomix is designed to be user-friendly. It comes with a touchscreen display that guides you step-by-step through recipes, making it simple for anyone to cook a wide variety of dishes.

Question 3: Can the Thermomix cook multiple dishes at the same time?

Yes, using the Varoma attachment, Thermomix can steam vegetables, proteins, or other foods while simultaneously cooking another dish in the main bowl.

Question 4: Does the Thermomix come with recipes?

Yes, the Thermomix comes with a Cook-Key, which provides access to thousands of recipes from the Thermomix Recipe Platform, Cookidoo.

Question 5: How often do I need to clean my Thermomix?

It's recommended to clean your Thermomix after each use. The self-cleaning function makes it easy. However, certain parts may need occasional deep cleaning.

Question 6: What's the warranty on a Thermomix?

The warranty on a Thermomix is 24 months for private use and 12 months for commercial use.

Question 7: What safety features does the Thermomix have?

The Thermomix has several safety features, including a locking system that ensures the lid is secure during operation, overheat protection, and a residual heat indicator.

Question 8: Are there any additional accessories I need to purchase in order to use the Thermomix?

No, you do not need to purchase any additional accessories to use the Thermomix. The device comes with all the necessary components for its operation, including a mixing bowl, a simmering basket, a spatula, and a Varoma (steaming attachment). However, there are optional accessories available for purchase that can enhance your Thermomix experience, such as additional mixing bowls or cutters.

Question 9: Can I buy Thermomix accessories separately?

Yes, various accessories such as the Varoma, additional mixing bowls, butterfly whisk, and spatulas can be purchased separately.

Question 10: Is the Thermomix cost-effective?

While the upfront cost may seem high, the Thermomix replaces several kitchen appliances and could save you money over time. Additionally, it can help reduce food waste and save time in food preparation and cooking.

Question 11: Can the Thermomix help with dietary restrictions or preferences?

Yes, the Thermomix's recipe platform, Cookidoo, offers a wide range of recipes catering to various dietary needs such as gluten-free, dairy-free, vegetarian, vegan, low-carb, and more.

Question 12: Can children use the Thermomix?

While the Thermomix is user-friendly, it's recommended that children use it only under adult supervision due to the machine's sharp blades and heating function.

Question 13: Can I manually control the Thermomix, or do I always have to use a recipe?

Yes, you can manually control the Thermomix. The device allows for manual setting of time, temperature, and speed for your personalized recipes.
